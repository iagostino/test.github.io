Hi! I am Isabel Agostino, a Ph.D. candidate in [Industrial Engineering & Operations Research](https://ieor.berkeley.edu/) at the [University of California, Berkeley](https://www.berkeley.edu/). My advisor is [Thibaut Mastrolia](https://mastrolia.ieor.berkeley.edu/), and I am fortunate to have also previously worked with [Alper Atamtürk](https://atamturk.ieor.berkeley.edu/). Prior to beginning my Ph.D., I worked as a business process optimization consultant for [West Monroe](https://www.westmonroe.com/). I graduated *summa cum laude* and phi beta kappa with a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics from [William & Mary](https://www.wm.edu/), where I also studied economics and chemistry.

## Research Interests
---
I am currently working on stochastic control, especially its role in designing contracts under uncertainty and threat from cyber attacks. Recently, I have been working on:
<ul>
  <li>Numerical methods for approximation of unsolvable free-boundary gradient-constrained PDEs with jumps</li>
  <li>Valuation of energy contracts with cyberattacks and random stopping</li>
</ul>
